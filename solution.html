The Problem & Our Solution

The Challenge: Inefficiencies of a Manual System

Quick Stock's reliance on a manual, spreadsheet-based system created significant operational challenges that hindered growth and efficiency. Key issues included:

Inaccurate Stock Levels

Manual data entry errors resulted in incorrect inventory counts, causing discrepancies between recorded and actual stock.

Stockouts & Overstocking

A lack of real-time visibility led to frequent stockouts of popular items and overstocking of slow-moving items, tying up capital.

Inefficient Processes

Time-consuming processes for stock-taking, sales recording, and generating reorder reports reduced overall employee productivity.

Poor Decision-Making

Management lacked the timely and accurate data needed to make informed purchasing and sales strategy decisions.

Our Solution: Project Goals & Objectives

The new Quick Stock Inventory Management System (QIMS) is designed to directly address these challenges by delivering:

  • ✔ Real-time tracking of inventory levels for all products.
  • ✔ Automation of the sales process to instantly update inventory counts.
  • ✔ Automated low-stock alerts and suggested purchase orders.
  • ✔ Over 99% data accuracy by eliminating manual entry errors.
  • ✔ Comprehensive reports on sales trends, stock levels, and product performance.
  • ✔ A 15% reduction in inventory carrying costs and elimination of top-item stockouts.
