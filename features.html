System Features

Manager Dashboard

A central dashboard provides a high-level overview of key inventory metrics at a glance, including total sales, low-stock items, and recent activity, enabling quick and informed management decisions.

Product Information Management

QIMS allows managers to easily add, edit, view, and disable products in the inventory. This feature centralizes product data, ensuring consistency and accuracy across the system.

Point-of-Sale (POS) Functionality

An intuitive sales processing interface for cashiers to scan products, process transactions, and automatically update inventory counts in real-time, reducing checkout times and eliminating manual entry errors.

Reporting and Analytics

Generate comprehensive reports on sales trends, top-selling products, and current stock levels. This data-driven insight helps optimize purchasing strategies and maximize profitability.
